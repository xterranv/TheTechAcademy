How to Train your Dragons - Dragon List

Name Class Features
Night Fury Strike Black in color. Cat-like eyes Ear-like appendages Nubs and tendrils protruding from head Secondary wings and has tail fins
Light Fury Strike White in color. Has Cat-like eyes Ear-like appendages Nubs and tendrils protruding from head Secondary wings, and has tail fins
Night Light Strike Black and white. Cat-like eyes Ear-like appendages Nubs and tendrils protruding from head Secondary wings and tail fins
Triple Stryke Strike 3 stingers, attached to it's tail. This dragon is hard to train for it's aggressive behavior. Colors vary, but mainly Brown and yellow
Skrill Strike Considered the Most feared dragon as they can summon lightning more powerful than any natural storm.
Deathgripper Strike Deathgrippers are a monstrous species with their protruding tusks, foreclaw pincers, and poisonous clubbed tails. Traveling in packs like wild dogs, Deathgrippers enjoy hunting for prey and should be avoided at all costs.
